Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Effective Date: June 07, 2026  ·  Corbel Group LLC d/b/a answered.  ·  hello@getanswered.co

The short version: We only collect the information you give us through our contact form. We use it to respond to your inquiry and, if you become a client, to deliver our service. We don’t sell your data. We don’t run ads. We don’t share your information with anyone except the service providers that help us operate.

1. Who We Are

answered. is a contractor operations service operated by Corbel Group LLC, a Wyoming limited liability company. References to “we,” “us,” or “Company” in this policy refer to Corbel Group LLC d/b/a answered.

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through our website at getanswered.co and through our service delivery platform. It does not apply to information collected about our contractor clients’ customers, which is governed by our Service Agreement.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your first name
  • Your business name
  • Your trade or industry type
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • How many calls you estimate you miss per week

We do not require you to create an account or provide a password to use our website.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our server may collect standard technical information when you visit, including your IP address, browser type, referring URL, and pages visited. This information is used solely for security and basic analytics purposes and is not linked to your personal identity.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for the website to function. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use the site.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry via phone, email, or text message
  • Determine whether our services are a fit for your business
  • Schedule a discovery conversation if appropriate
  • Deliver our services if you become a client
  • Send you information about answered. that may be relevant to your business

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to our business relationship with you.

4. Text Message Communications and TCPA

By submitting your phone number through our contact form, you expressly consent to receive text messages from answered. regarding your inquiry and our services. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.

We comply with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and applicable state telemarketing laws. We will only contact you in connection with your inquiry or our business relationship. We will honor opt-out requests promptly and will not send marketing messages to individuals who have requested to stop receiving them.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Formspree — our contact form processor, which transmits form submissions to our inbox. Formspree’s privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.
  • GoHighLevel (GHL) — our client relationship management platform, which stores contact and communication records for active clients and prospects. GHL’s privacy policy is available at gohighlevel.com/privacy-policy.
  • Google Workspace — our email and document platform, operated under Google’s enterprise privacy terms.
  • Law enforcement or legal process — if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our business or others.

All third-party service providers we use are required to handle your data in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

6. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and inquiry records for up to two (2) years from the date of submission, unless you request deletion earlier. If you become a client, your data is retained for the duration of our service relationship and for a period of two (2) years following termination, after which it is permanently deleted.

You may request deletion of your information at any time by emailing hello@getanswered.co. We will process deletion requests within thirty (30) days.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of text message communications by replying STOP
  • Opt out of email communications by using the unsubscribe link or emailing us directly

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@getanswered.co. We do not discriminate against individuals for exercising their privacy rights.

California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, and to opt out of any sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To submit a CCPA request, contact us at hello@getanswered.co.

8. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. These measures include encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS), access controls limiting who can view your data, and confidentiality requirements for personnel who handle client information.

No method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are intended for business owners and are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Effective Date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify active clients via email. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
